Our Commitment to Privacy

At ToolSky, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information. We are committed to transparency and ensuring your data remains yours.

We never store your files. All uploaded files are processed temporarily and deleted immediately.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve our services:

  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about which tools are used most
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Contact information: If you email us at toolsky67@gmail.com, we keep your email only to respond

What we DON'T collect: We never collect names, addresses, payment info, or store any uploaded files.

Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze traffic through Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not work properly.

Google Analytics collects anonymous data about page views and tool usage. This helps us understand what features to improve.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits.
  • Google Analytics: Tracks anonymous usage statistics.
  • Font Awesome & AOS: For icons and animations (these don't track you).

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Request what data we have about you (spoiler: very little)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of cookies

To exercise these rights, email us at toolsky67@gmail.com.

Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns or questions, please contact us:

Email: toolsky67@gmail.com

Response time: Within 24-48 hours
